The ATLAS Women in Design (Wind) group is aimed at increasing participation of women in the creative technology and design fields.

Learn More
ATLAS Wind is an ACM-W (Association for Computing Machinery - Women in computing) chapter within the ATLAS Institute at the University of Colorado at Boulder.
We support, encourage, and empower women to be engaged in computing, technology, design, engineering, and creative fields.
